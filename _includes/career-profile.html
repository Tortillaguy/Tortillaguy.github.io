About Me

Hello!


Since learning C++ and Java courses in high school, I've been fascinated with mixing code and visuals. At a college internship, I began to produce live projection mapped shows for both upscale events and local warehouse concerts. At hackathons I was often responsible for the front end experience and interactive elements of the application. My favorite software to work with is Unity, Touchdesigner, and ThreeJS; though I often experiment with other applications outside my wheelhouse.


I'm always to open to hearing out new opportunities and adventures. If interested please feel free to send a note.
