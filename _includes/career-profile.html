About Me

Hello!

As both a developer and artist, I've been fascinated with mixing code and visuals. I'm a lover of live events and have produced projection mapped displays for settings anywhere from upscale to warehouse. In hackathons and team projects, I excel at designing the front end experience and interactive elements of the application. My favorite software to work with is Unity, Touchdesigner, and ThreeJS; though I often experiment with other applications outside my wheelhouse.

I'm always to open to hearing out new opportunities and adventures. If interested, feel free to send a note.
