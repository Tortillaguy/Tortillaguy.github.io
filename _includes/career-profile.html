About Me

I'm a technoculturalist with a great passion for creative coding. As a student taking traditional C++ and Java courses, I independently began toying with Processing and Touchdesigner to create my own graphics applications. My early career as an audio video technician gave way to a better, professional understanding of computer hardware, sound, lighting, and data networks. And with that I began creating my own interactive installations and VJ sets for clients and friends. Now, I work predominantly on developing augmented and virtual reality applications using Unreal and Unity, designed for Vive, Kinect, Hololens, etc. Ultimately I enjoy creating a unique user experience and have adapted to a diverse range of programming languages and concepts in doing so.
